01 — The problem

Something is broken between us and God

We all do wrong. That wrongdoing — the Bible calls it sin — creates a wall between us and God. Nobody is exempt.

For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God. Romans 3:23
02 — The consequence

That separation has a cost

Left unfixed, sin leads to permanent separation from God. But the same verse that says that also offers a way out — a free gift.

The wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord. Romans 6:23
03 — The solution

God sent Jesus to pay what we owed

Jesus took the punishment for our sin when he died on the cross — then rose from the dead. He did it before you ever asked. That is what love looks like.

While we were still sinners, Christ died for us. Romans 5:8
04 — Your move

Turn, believe, and be saved

Repentance means turning — away from sin and toward God. Not perfection, just a genuine change of direction. Pair that with trusting Jesus as Lord and the door is open right now.

Declare "Jesus is Lord" and believe God raised him from the dead, and you will be saved. Romans 10:9
